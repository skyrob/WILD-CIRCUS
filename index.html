WILD CIRCUS - An Animal-Free Circus !


At WILD CIRCUS we have done the choice to ban any animal in our show.


We refuse animal exploitation for human amusement.

We believe in intelligence and compassion of our spectators.

After all, why would you pay to see miserable, confused (and potentially dangerous) wild animals trot out a tired routine that they’ve been forced to learn through beatings and other abuse when you could watch exuberant human acrobats soar through the air, fantastic magicians flirting with paranormal or knife thrower death-defying of their wife partner ?


Ladies and gentlemen, boys and girls, children of all ages, are you ready for one of the greatest circus show ?!!!

Then, step right up and treat your family, your friends, or just yourself to a circus that features only willing human performers!

Mr. FLIM

Dangerous exhibition.
Mr. FLIM is a fire eater with over 15 years' performing experience.
He has confounded and entertained England Royal Courts audience with feats of heat resistance and fire manipulation.
He ejects thousands of brilliant sparks from his mouth (technique, also known as Chinese Fire Eating). Find other expressions of his art as finger transfer (transfering the flame from one torch to the other with fingers), flaming cotton ball extinction (putting out a cotton ball) and many other tremendous tricks.
